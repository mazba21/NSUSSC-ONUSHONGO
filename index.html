ONUSHONGO

Reconnecting generations through empathy. A flagship initiative supporting the resilience of our senior citizens in Gazipur.

The Objective

Honoring the roots that raised us.

Launched on 12th April 2019 at Monipur, Gazipur, "Onushanga" seeks to revive the feeling of being loved for those residing in old age homes.

We believe rejecting one's roots is the ultimate sorrow. Through interaction, storytelling, and music, our volunteers provide a temporary escape from isolation for over 217 inmates.
